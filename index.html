Our Story

Baked With Love & Tradition

Ibee Resto Cafe started with one simple dream — to bring freshly baked Indian delicacies to every doorstep. We blend traditional Indian baking techniques with modern cafe vibes, creating a unique experience you won't find anywhere else.

From our signature breads to indulgent pastries, every item is prepared fresh daily. With branches across Tiruchengode, Sankagiri, and Kootapalli — we're always near you!
